Hey there!

I'm Andrew, an alumnus of the University of Toronto. I studied Computer Science in my degree and have worked with various platforms and technologies, such as the traditional PHP (and HTML/CSS/JS/SQL) stack, Java, C and some NodeJS + React. I also have some basic experience with Linux systems administration. If you'd like to see some of my projects, visit my GitHub page and have a look.

In my free time, I enjoy coding, travelling, watching the occasional video or two, sometimes cooking something new and trying new food.

Recently, I completed a IT quality assurance internship at the City of Toronto. Eventually, I hope to return to school to complete a master's degree in a topic of interest.

I'm always happy to speak with someone new, so please feel free to contact me, whether you're talking business or just want to say hi :)
